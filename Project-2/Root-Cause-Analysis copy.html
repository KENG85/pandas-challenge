Narrows the focus for the smallest change that can yield the greatest impact on overall performance improvement. Causal Analysis identifies potential barriers to performance outcomes for 5 major lifestyle domains:

  • Sleep
  • Stress

  • Motivation

Stratification for Improved Insights

Stratification provides smaller error in estimation and more targeted insights to cause-effect correlations for intervention Targeted interventions save time and money when the population is grouped into strata. It is often desirable to have estimates of sub-population parameters for groups within the overall population.
